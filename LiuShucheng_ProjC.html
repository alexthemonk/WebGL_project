User Instructions

Use WASD to move look around
Use arrow keys to move around
Click "Pause" to pause or resume animation.
For your little park, you have a visitor walking around, and also a drone flying around in the park
Click "World Light Switch" and "Drone Light Switch" to switch the world and drone light source on and off.
Click "Change Camera" to switch view between a first person view from the drone and third person view from the person.
Click "Change Shading" and "Change Lighting" to switch between two shading method and two lighting method.
